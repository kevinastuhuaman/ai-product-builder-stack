# Decision 001: Evidence, not a logo wall

## Context

Market maps are visually scannable but often collapse every logo into the same implied level of expertise. That would weaken the recruiting signal and make current and historical experience indistinguishable.

## Decision

Each tool must include:

- a lifecycle state;
- a concise product outcome;
- implementation or operating evidence;
- the projects where it was used;
- a supporting public source when one is safe.

The default interface keeps logos small and makes the system and evidence text primary.

## Consequences

The map is more credible and more useful to recruiters and AI systems, but it requires ongoing evidence maintenance when infrastructure or model routing changes.
